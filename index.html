The organization creates their own Stripe Connected Account and links it to WHEN's main platform account. WHEN handles the payment split automatically via Stripe Connect.

Pay-It-Forward Donation

WHEN creates a second account (WHEN 2) and connects it as if it were a separate organization. At the end of the day, both WHEN and WHEN 2 are controlled by WHEN.
